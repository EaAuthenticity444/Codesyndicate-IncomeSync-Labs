## 📊 Financial Distribution Model

### Monthly Revenue Allocation

**Total Monthly Revenue** (example: $100,000)

```
70% → Contributor Payouts ($70,000)
   ├─ 50% → Active developers ($35,000)
   ├─ 20% → Maintainers & reviewers ($14,000)
   └─ 0% → Contingency reserve

20% → Operations & Scaling ($20,000)
   ├─ 10% → Infrastructure & hosting
   ├─ 5% → Marketing & sales
   ├─ 3% → Customer support
   └─ 2% → Admin & legal

10% → Reinvestment & Growth ($10,000)
   ├─ 6% → R&D (Labs projects)
   ├─ 3% → Team expansion
   └─ 1% → Emergency fund
```

## 💳 Contributor Payout System

### Point System
- **Commit**: +2 points
- **Code Review**: +10 points
- **Bug Fix**: +15 points
- **Feature**: +25 points
- **Documentation**: +10 points
- **Issue Resolution**: +20 points

### Monthly Calculation
```
Individual Monthly Payout = 
(Individual Points / Total Team Points) × (Total Revenue × 0.70)
```

### Example
- Total revenue: $100,000
- Total team points: 2,000
- Your points: 400 (20%)
- Your payout: (400/2000) × $70,000 = **$14,000**

## 🎯 Payout Schedule

### Weekly
- Friday: Point totals calculated
- Friday EOD: Confirmations sent

### Monthly
- 1st-5th: Final reconciliation
- 5th-10th: Payouts processed
- 15th: Team financial report published

### Minimum Payout Threshold
- **$100**: Minimum monthly earnings to process
- Below threshold: Rolls over to next month
