## 💰 Reward System

- **Commit**: +2 points
- **Code Review**: +10 points
- **Bug Fix**: +15 points
- **Feature**: +25 points
- **Documentation**: +10 points
- **Issue Resolution**: +20 points

**Monthly Payout**: Total Revenue × (Individual Points / Total Points)
